About Me

Hey! I'm Christine Mi. I'm from San Diego, California and I just graduated from Yale University with a degree in Economics. I make art in my free time and I love all mediums, digital or traditional.

I'm in love with making Snapchat art and currently work with as a freelance Snapchat artist and strategist, where I work with brands on social media marketing campaigns on the platform.

I'm an avid traveler (23 countries so far and counting!) and I'm obsessed with animals (currently really into birds). I CAN'T WAIT TO SEE SHARKS AND RIDE OSTRICHES.
